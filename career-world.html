About Career World

Career World Coaching Centre is a premier educational welfare initiative operated by the South Central Railway Women's Welfare Organisation (SCoRWWO) in Vijayawada. We specialize in providing high-quality coaching for RRB, BANKING, SSC, ECET and various other competitive exams.

At Career World, we take great pride in offering top-quality education at incredibly affordable rates. This commitment to excellence and affordability makes us the preferred choice for students across the region. We believe that every aspiring student deserves access to quality coaching, no matter their budget. Our program not only improves academic knowledge and exam strategies but also nurtures the core skills needed to excel in competitive environments with personalized guidance every step of the way.

Why Choose Career World?

Competitive Exam Specializations

We specialize in comprehensive preparation for major state and national level competitive recruitment boards:

  • Railway Recruitment Board (RRB): NTPC, Group D, ALP, and Technical Exams.
  • Banking Exams: IBPS, SBI PO & Clerk, RBI Assistant.
  • Staff Selection Commission (SSC): CGL, CHSL, MTS, and GD.
  • Engineering Common Entrance Test (ECET): Detailed coaching for technical streams.
  • Other state-level competitive and recruitment examinations.
