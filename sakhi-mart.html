Convenient Shopping for Passengers & Railway Employees

About Sakhi Mart

Sakhi Mart is a retail shopping outlet associated with the railway commercial area serving passengers, railway employees, and local residents. It provides a variety of daily essentials, snacks, beverages, packaged foods, personal care products, and travel necessities.

Its convenient location near the railway premises makes it useful for passengers looking for quick purchases before or after their journey. The store contributes to passenger convenience by offering easily accessible products at one location.

Role in South Coast Railway

The South Coast Railway zone focuses on improving passenger amenities and station infrastructure. Retail stores like Sakhi Mart enhance the overall travel experience by providing essential services within or near railway premises.

Location

Near Vijayawada Junction Railway Station, one of the busiest railway stations in India, serving thousands of passengers every day.

Short Description

Sakhi Mart is a convenient shopping outlet near the South Coast Railway premises, offering groceries, snacks, beverages, personal care products, and travel essentials for passengers and visitors. Its accessible location helps make railway travel more comfortable and convenient.
