Latest Events

Recent activities and updates from the South Central Railway Women's Welfare Organisation.

24 May

Hydration Drive

The Hydration Drive initiative was organized to provide clean drinking water and refreshments to passengers and railway staff during the summer season. The event promoted health awareness, heat-safety practices, and community welfare across railway stations. Through this effort, SCoRWWO reinforced its commitment to public service and passenger well-being.

29 JAN

NUKKAND NATAK And Distribution of sanitary napkins

A cultural event was organized to promote awareness about women's health and hygiene, along with the distribution of sanitary napkins to underprivileged women.
