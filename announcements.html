Important Announcements

Critical advisories, timetables changes, and official notifications.

10 May

Launching the South Coast Railway website

South Coast Railway is pleased to launch its official website, providing passengers with easy access to railway services, train information, announcements, and updates. The platform is designed to enhance user experience through a simple, accessible, and efficient digital interface, helping travelers stay informed and connected.

29 Jan

Integrated School Management System launched for Jack & Jill School

Jack and Jill School introduces its Integrated School Management System to simplify academic and administrative processes. The platform enables efficient communication, seamless record management, and improved access to essential school information.

05 May

General Manager to launch website and school system

The General Manager is set to officially launch the South Coast Railway website and the Integrated School Management System for Jack and Jill School. These initiatives mark a significant step toward digital transformation, enhancing accessibility, efficiency, and user experience for both passengers and educational stakeholders.
