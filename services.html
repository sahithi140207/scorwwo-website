SOUTH COAST RAILWAY WOMEN'S WELFARE ORGANIZATION

Services

The South Coast Railway Women’s Welfare Organisation (SCoRWWO), Vijayawada Division, has rendered remarkable welfare, educational, healthcare, humanitarian, and social services for railway employees, women staff, children, specially-abled persons, and society for many years.

Welfare Services for Railway Employees

SCoRWWO continuously works for the welfare and wellbeing of railway employees and their families through:

  • Employee support programs
  • Family welfare activities
  • Recreational and cultural programs
  • Women staff support initiatives
  • Welfare camps and motivational activities

Railway officials have appreciated SCoRWWO for rendering “yeomen services” and helping employees during difficult situations and natural calamities.

Women Empowerment Activities

SCoRWWO conducts several programs to encourage and empower women employees, including:

  • International Women’s Day celebrations
  • Sports competitions
  • Cultural events
  • Leadership encouragement
  • Women awareness programs
  • Workplace welfare improvements

At the Diesel Loco Shed, Vijayawada, SCoRWWO renovated and inaugurated the Ladies’ Rest Room for women employees and organized sports and cultural events for staff welfare.

Educational and Skill Development Services

SCoRWWO runs and supports several educational and developmental centers in Vijayawada, including:

  • E-World
  • Career World
  • Jack & Jill School
  • Asha Kiran

These institutions provide:

  • Computer education
  • Internship opportunities
  • Coaching and training
  • Child education
  • Personality development
  • Rehabilitation and therapy support

SCoRWWO also launched Career World Coaching Centre for educational support and student development.

Services for Specially-Abled Children

One of the most appreciated services of SCRWWO is the support provided to specially-abled children through:

  • Autism care
  • Down syndrome support
  • Speech therapy
  • Hearing impairment support
  • Life skill training
  • Rehabilitation programs

Asha Kiran serves specially challenged children with developmental disorders and conducts rehabilitation and learning activities.

Student Internship and Social Service Programs

SCoRWWO introduced social-service internship programs for college students to develop:

  • Social responsibility
  • Leadership skills
  • Compassion and empathy
  • Humanitarian service mindset

Students are trained to interact with specially-abled children through:

  • Educational support activities
  • Communication and interaction sessions
  • Therapy assistance programs
  • Creative and recreational activities
  • Social awareness initiatives

Healthcare and Medical Services

SCoRWWO has contributed significantly to healthcare improvements by:

  • Conducting health awareness programs
  • Organizing hygiene awareness sessions
  • Supporting railway hospitals with medical equipment
  • Promoting women’s health initiatives

SCoRWWO donated advanced physiotherapy equipment worth several lakhs to Divisional Railway Hospital, Vijayawada, improving rehabilitation and physiotherapy services for patients.

Menstrual Hygiene and Women Health Initiatives

SCRWWO implemented important women hygiene services at Vijayawada Railway Station by installing:

  • Coin-operated sanitary napkin dispensers
  • Sanitary napkin incinerators

These facilities were introduced for women passengers and women railway employees to improve menstrual hygiene awareness and accessibility.

The organization also:

  • Distributed eco-friendly sanitary napkins
  • Conducted awareness sessions on menstrual hygiene
  • Encouraged women health education

Humanitarian and Relief Services

SCoRWWO is known for responding during:

  • Natural calamities
  • Emergencies
  • Employee distress situations

South Central Railway officials publicly appreciated the organization for extending humanitarian support and relief services whenever required.

Recognition and Encouragement Programs

SCoRWWO regularly honors:

  • Women employees
  • Group-D railway staff
  • Frontline railway workers
  • Dedicated service personnel

The organization felicitated gangmen, trolleymen, gatemen, and other field staff for their continuous hard work and service, especially during difficult conditions and the pandemic period.

Cultural and Community Development

SCoRWWO also promotes:

  • Music programs
  • Cultural events
  • Community gatherings
  • Employee family bonding activities

Special cultural programs were organized to raise funds for welfare and social development activities.

Overall, SCoRWWO Vijayawada has become an important welfare pillar within South Central Railway by combining social service, employee welfare, women empowerment, education, healthcare, and humanitarian support activities for the benefit of railway families and society.
