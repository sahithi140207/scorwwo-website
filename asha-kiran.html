About Asha Kiran

Asha Kiran Centre in Vijayawada is a special welfare institution run by the South Central Railway Women’s Welfare Organisation (SCoRWWO). It provides education, therapy, rehabilitation, and life-skill training to children with developmental and intellectual disabilities.

The center focuses on creating a friendly and safe learning environment where differently-abled children receive dedicated care, creative education, and therapy to help them improve their abilities and lead more independent and confident lives.

Children Supported

Asha Kiran works dedicatedly with children affected by various developmental and special needs conditions:

Training & Skill-Development Activities

Students at Asha Kiran participate in active learning, creative expression, and practical training designed to unlock their inner potential:

  • Reading and writing practice for cognitive growth
  • Interactive storytelling sessions to improve focus and imagination
  • Drawing and painting to encourage creative expression
  • Handicraft and vocational activities for motor skill development
  • Dance, music, and cultural programs to build confidence
  • Life-skill training focusing on self-reliance in daily tasks
  • Group interaction sessions for social and behavioral enhancement

Importance of Asha Kiran

Asha Kiran plays an important role in supporting specially-abled children and their families. The center focuses not only on basic education but also on comprehensive rehabilitation, confidence-building, and social interaction.

By providing college students with service opportunities and giving children a positive community space, it prepares differently-abled youth for a better, more supportive, and integrated future in society.

Main Objectives

  • Help children become more independent in daily life.
  • Improve verbal, non-verbal communication, and social skills.
  • Support healthy emotional and behavioral development.
  • Encourage creativity through arts, crafts, music, and dance.
  • Provide a friendly, safe, and positive learning environment.
