About the School

Jack & Jill School (EM), Vijayawada, is a well-known English-medium school managed by the South Central Railway Women's Welfare Organisation (SCoRWWO). Established in 2006, it aims to provide quality education while nurturing discipline, creativity, leadership skills, and moral values. The school encourages students to participate in academics, sports, and cultural activities to ensure holistic development. It is known for providing affordable, high-standard education with a deep focus on character building and personality development.

School Activities & Recognition

Beyond textbooks, the school nurtures student talents by regularly organizing and celebrating major events:

  • Independence Day & Republic Day Celebrations
  • Teachers' Day and Children's Day programs
  • Inter-school sports tournaments and athletic competitions
  • Cultural programs, annual day events, and festivals
  • Extracurricular talent competitions (Drawing, Essay Writing, and Debating)
Teachers and staff have been highly recognized by SCoRWWO for their contribution to quality education and student development.

Admission Details

Admissions are generally available from pre-primary classes to Class X, depending on seat availability. Parents are required to submit:

  • Official Birth Certificate
  • Previous Academic Progress Report
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Recent Passport-size photographs
  • Student and Parent identity documents
Admission enquiries can be made directly at the school office.
