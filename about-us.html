About Us

Dedicated to public welfare, passenger support, and railway community empowerment.

History

South Coast Railway Women's Welfare Organization (SCoRWWO) was established as the 18th railway zone of Indian Railways to strengthen railway administration and improve transportation services in Andhra Pradesh. The zone was announced on 27 February 2019 with its headquarters at Visakhapatnam. The creation of South Coast Railway marked an important milestone in the development of railway infrastructure in the state.

The zone was formed with the objective of providing better operational efficiency, enhanced passenger amenities, faster project implementation, and improved freight management. Since its inception, South Coast Railway has focused on modernization, safety, digital transformation, and customer-centric services.

Our Network

South Coast Railway Women's Welfare Organization serves as a vital transportation network connecting important cities, towns, industrial centers, ports, and tourist destinations across Andhra Pradesh and neighboring regions. The railway network facilitates the movement of millions of passengers and large volumes of freight every year.

Major routes connect key locations such as Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, Guntur, Guntakal, and several other important destinations. The network supports economic activities by providing efficient transportation for industries, agriculture, trade, and tourism.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading railway zone that delivers safe, efficient, reliable, and sustainable transportation services while contributing to national development and customer satisfaction.

Mission

  • Ensure the highest standards of safety in railway operations.
  • Provide punctual, comfortable, and affordable travel experiences.
  • Improve operational efficiency through modern technology.
  • Enhance passenger amenities and customer support services.
  • Promote environmentally sustainable railway practices.
  • Support economic growth through efficient freight transportation.
  • Strengthen connectivity between cities, industries, ports, and tourist destinations.
  • Encourage innovation and continuous improvement across all railway activities.
