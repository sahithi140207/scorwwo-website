Vijayawada Division

South Coast Railway Women’s Welfare Organisation

The South Coast Railway Women’s Welfare Organisation (SCoRWWO) is a registered welfare and social service organization working under South Coast Railway for the welfare of railway employees, women staff, children, and railway families in the Vijayawada Division.

About SCoRWWO Vijayawada Division

The South Coast Railway Women’s Welfare Organisation (SCoRWWO) actively works towards the welfare and development of railway employees and their families. The organization conducts educational, healthcare, cultural, and social welfare activities across the Vijayawada Division. Vijayawada Division is one of the most important railway divisions under South Coast Railway and plays a major role in railway operations and passenger services.

Main Objectives

  • Welfare of railway employees and their families
  • Women empowerment and support activities
  • Educational and healthcare initiatives
  • Child welfare and special-needs support
  • Skill development and cultural activities
  • Employee recreational and social programs

Important Welfare Activities

Educational Support

Running institutions and welfare centers for children and specially challenged students.

Healthcare
Services

Supporting railway hospitals with advanced medical and physiotherapy equipment.

Women Empowerment

Organizing awareness programs, sports, cultural events, and empowerment activities.

Passenger Amenities

Improving facilities for women passengers and railway staff at railway stations.

Institutions & Welfare Centers

  • E-World
  • Career World
  • Jack & Jill School
  • Asha Kiran (for specially challenged children)
  • Canteen
  • Sakhi Mart

Recent Welfare Initiatives

  • Installation of sanitary napkin vending machines and incinerators at Vijayawada Railway Station.
  • Donation of advanced physiotherapy equipment to Divisional Railway Hospital, Vijayawada.
  • International Women’s Day celebrations honoring women employees at Diesel Loco Shed.
  • Welfare support during emergencies and employee assistance programs.

Leadership

The organization is generally headed by the spouse of the Divisional Railway Manager (DRM). Over the years, different presidents have contributed towards employee welfare and social development. One of the recent presidents mentioned in reports is Mrs. Versha Sonakiya.

Role in Employee Welfare

SCoRWWO plays an important humanitarian and social role in the railway ecosystem by supporting women employees, providing educational opportunities to children, conducting healthcare and awareness programs, and encouraging community development among railway families. The organization has been appreciated by senior railway officials for its welfare services and social contribution.
