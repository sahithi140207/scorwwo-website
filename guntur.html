Guntur Division

The South Coast Railway Women’s Welfare Organisation (SCoRWWO) is a registered welfare and social service organization working under South Coast Railway for the welfare of railway employees, women staff, children, and railway families in the Guntur Division.

About Guntur Division

The Guntur Division is an important railway division that serves the central region of Andhra Pradesh. The division plays a crucial role in providing rail connectivity to major agricultural, commercial, educational, and cultural centers within the state. With its strategic location, the division acts as a key link between coastal Andhra Pradesh and other regions of South India. Agriculture forms the backbone of the region served by the division. The Guntur Division supports the transportation of agricultural commodities such as tobacco, chillies, cotton, rice, pulses, and food grains to various parts of the country. This efficient freight network helps farmers, traders, and industries by ensuring timely movement of goods. The division also handles cement, fertilizers, industrial products, and other commercial cargo, contributing to regional economic development. The division serves several important railway stations including Guntur Junction, Tenali Junction, Narasaraopet, Repalle, and Macherla. These stations facilitate passenger travel for business, education, tourism, and religious purposes. The division continues to enhance railway infrastructure, improve station facilities, and strengthen operational efficiency to meet the growing transportation needs of passengers and industries.
